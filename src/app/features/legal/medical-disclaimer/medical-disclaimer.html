Notice to Users

The information provided on this website is for general educational purposes only and is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

01

Professional Consultation Required

We strongly recommend booking a professional consultation at one of our centers before purchasing any hearing corrective device. Our senior audiologists will ensure that the device is appropriate for your specific clinical needs and is properly fitted to your unique ear anatomy.

02

Online Screening Limitations

Our rapid online hearing test is a screening tool designed to provide a preliminary assessment of your hearing. It is not a substitute for a comprehensive diagnostic audiometry test performed by a certified specialist in a controlled clinical environment using clinical-grade equipment.

03

Device Performance Variation

The performance of hearing aids can vary significantly based on:

  • Individual ear anatomy
  • Degree of hearing loss
  • Environmental acoustic factors
  • Consistency of usage
